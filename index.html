Biography

I am a scientist in the Machine Intelligence team at Seeing Machines, where I am proud to be contributing to the development of life-saving technologies through the application of AI. My research encompasses a diverse range of areas including 3D vision, efficient machine learning, generative models, and domain adaptation, among others. I am passionate about exploring new and innovative approaches to solving complex technical challenges.
